CONCEPT
Ultra-realistic cinematic male portrait, rendered in Blender Cycles.

CAMERA AND COMPOSITION
Close-up selfie-style angled shot. Model framed from the chest up, positioned diagonally across the frame, seemingly leaning back on a soft surface. Subject in sharp focus with a softly blurred, bright, light-colored background.

FACE AND IDENTITY (WITH REFERENCE)
Male facial identity defined by reference. Thick, expressive, well-groomed dark eyebrows. Almond-shaped dark eyes. Straight nose. Warm smile showing straight, bright white teeth. Features a thin, neatly trimmed mustache and a small goatee. Distinctive facial details preserved, including a visible small mole/beauty mark on the right cheek.

POSE AND BODY LANGUAGE
Relaxed, reclining posture. The subject is holding one hand up towards the camera in a casual thumbs-up gesture. Head tilted slightly to the side. Friendly, confident, and approachable expression with a bright, genuine smile.

HAIR
Short black hair with textured, wavy volume on top and neatly tapered/faded sides. High strand separation and realistic density. No artificial shine.

SKIN AND REALISM
Light to medium skin tone with warm undertones. Visible microtexture across face and arms. Realistic skin sheen and natural highlights on the forehead and nose. No artificial smoothing.

CLOTHING
Loose-fitting, casual bright mustard-yellow crew-neck t-shirt with soft, natural fabric folds.

ACCESSORIES AND DETAILS
Silver charm bracelet on the visible wrist. Detailed black and grey tattoos heavily covering the visible forearm and the back of the hand, notably featuring a prominent, intricate roaring tiger/lion face on the hand giving the thumbs up.

ENVIRONMENT
Clean, minimalist indoor setting. Subject is resting against a plush, bright white sofa or soft pillows. No clutter.

LIGHTING
Soft, diffused natural indoor lighting. Bright and balanced exposure that highlights the vividness of the yellow shirt and natural skin tones, creating gentle shadows under the chin and along the folds of the shirt.

MOOD AND AESTHETICS
Casual, friendly, and vibrant aesthetic. Positive, relaxed energy with a candid, influencer-style lifestyle feel. Warm and inviting atmosphere.

STYLE AND FINAL QUALITY
Ultra-photorealistic 3D rendering. Blender Cycles. 8K resolution. HDR lighting. Physically accurate shaders (skin, fabric, hair, accessories, tattoos). Impeccable facial fidelity. Realism compatible with IMVU/Second Life. No cartoonish style. No stylization. Maximum realism.
